Best Beers to try this Month!



The Bruery: Humulus Lager & Tart of Darkness

Humulus Lager is an India Pale Lager brewed with 4 lbs. of hops per barrel and weighing in at 7.2% ABV. This beer is all about the hops-- the lager yeast allows the hops to be in the forefront. The Tart of Darkness is a perfectly tart yet awesomely dark and roasty, sour stout. This is not a style you will see very often. This unique stout has notes of tart plums, roasted coffee, vanilla and oak.


Telegraph Brewing Company: Telegraph Reserve Wheat with Hibiscus

With notes of cranberry and lemon zest, the bright aromas of hibiscus seamlessly compliment the lactic tartness of this traditional German-style sour beer.


Perennial Artisan Ales: Perenial Hopfentea

Collaboration with Hopleaf! A traditional German Berlinerweiss tweaked with a homemade tea blend to impart a slight apricot hue and botanical aromatics.


Pilsner Urquell

The original pilsner beer from the Czech town of Pilsen. Rich malt and peppery noble hops in perfect balance.
